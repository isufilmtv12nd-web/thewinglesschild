故事大綱 SYNOPSIS
一個雨天，年幼的 Evan 和母親撐著紅色的傘來到福利院。母親滿身傷痕，聲稱會回來接他，但轉身離去，留下 Evan 孤單一人。

他輾轉於保母家和福利院之間，因語言不通而被孤立，唯一的安慰是母親留下的舊布偶。Evan 渴望被看見與關懷，卻一次次遭遇冷漠和排斥。

在一次被欺負的事件中，Evan 遇見前來整理弟弟遺物的靜瀾。靜瀾是一名大體化妝師，個性疏離，與人保持距離。Evan 對她產生好奇並尾隨離開福利院。靜瀾起初排斥，但在數次互動後逐漸接納他，並讓他暫時與自己共同生活；靜瀾也在 Evan 身上尋找著弟弟的影子。在參與兩次葬禮以及遺體化妝的觀摩後，Evan 開始對自己的存在感到質疑，並渴望自己有天也能像這些死者一樣受到對待。

在大體工作室，Evan 對遺體化妝產生濃厚的興趣。他偷偷模仿靜瀾的動作，幻想自己有一天也能擁有名字和身份。然而，靜瀾發現他在自己臉上化妝後，憤怒責罵，想到自己弟弟過世的事而痛苦不已，最後將 Evan 趕走。Evan 而後被遣返。

On a rainy day, young Evan arrives at an orphanage with his mother under a red umbrella. Covered in bruises, she promises that she will come back for him, but turns away and leaves, abandoning Evan alone.

He drifts between foster homes and the orphanage, isolated due to language barriers. His only comfort is an old rag doll left behind by his mother. Evan longs to be seen and cared for, yet is met time and again with indifference and rejection.

After being bullied, Evan encounters Jing-Lan, who has come to sort through her younger brother’s belongings. Jing-Lan is a mortician, emotionally distant and withdrawn. Evan becomes curious about her and follows her out of the orphanage. At first, she resists him, but after several encounters, she gradually allows him to stay with her temporarily. In Evan, she begins to see traces of her deceased brother. After attending two funerals and observing the process of postmortem makeup, Evan starts to question his own existence and develops a longing to one day receive the same attention and care as the deceased.

At the mortuary studio, Evan develops a strong fascination with postmortem makeup. He secretly imitates Jing-Lan’s techniques, imagining that one day he might have a name and an identity of his own. However, when Jing-Lan discovers that he has been applying makeup to his own face, she reacts with anger and scolds him. Overwhelmed by memories of her brother’s death, she breaks down and drives Evan away. Evan is later sent back.
