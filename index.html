有些人活著，卻從未被看見。

無國籍男孩 Evan，在被遺棄與忽視中成長，渴望被命名、被記住。直到他遇見同樣失去至親的大體化妝師靜瀾， 兩個孤獨的靈魂短暫相依，卻終將被命運分開。當世界選擇視而不見，你是否仍願意相信自己的存在？

Some lives pass by without ever being noticed.

Evan, a stateless boy, grows up abandoned and overlooked, longing to be named and remembered. Until he meets Jinglan, a mortuary makeup artist who has also lost someone dear, two solitary souls find brief solace in one another, only to be separated by fate. When the world chooses to look away, do you still believe in your own existence?
